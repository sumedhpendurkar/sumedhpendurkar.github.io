About Me

I am a first year masters of science student in computer science and enginering (CSCE) at Texas A&M University, College Station advised by Dr. Guni Sharon. My current research lies in the field of reinforcement learning, imitation learning. I am always motivated to learn more about optimization and machine learning theory.
Previously, I have worked as a research intern under the guidence of Dr. Biplab Banerjee at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Roorkee. Apart from research, I have worked on various projects on deep learning and participated in data science competitions and hackathons.
